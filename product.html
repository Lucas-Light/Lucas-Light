Global story

Drones are used often in sports. They provide certain camera angles that fixed cameras can not achieve. Because they fly they can make dynamic shots of players without obstructing them. The drone can be controlled from a distance. The pictures the drone makes can be send live to a computer that can save and edit those pictures. It gives unique possibilities and also offer unique opportunities to apply video arbitration.

Article

What problem does this solve

Fixed cameras can not allways get every action in the picture. Drones are much more flexible and because they can fly, they can get much better angles. The pictures form the drone are sharp and high quality. The viewer can watch a match as if he flies over the players also with some close ups. This will give great immersion and will lift the sport to the next level.

Article

How to use

In oder to use the drones, the operator has a panel with joysticks that he uses to control the movement and camera of the drone. He can speed up, slow sown, lift and lower the drone. Also diagonal movements are possible. The camera has an auto focus option, but can also be manually set and controlled. It's also possible to take snapshots instead of filming and it also has timer functions.

Article

Who is using this

The people that are using this new technology are often people that film matches in football or other sports inside stadiums. This is mainly because there is plenty of room to manouvre. However it is also used by amateurs that like to film natural environments. The drone can even be used for racing where the operator has a virtual reality headset on and have great immersion in whatever they are filming.

What can you expect

Whith these drones you can easlily get all actions on the playingfield in the picture. It has excellent moving and filming capabilities that are high quality. The drone can be controlled from far away so you can make impressive overview shots. It is also able to hoover on a fixed spot if desired, or quickly move in all directions. Future from this innovation

In the future the flexibility of the drones will keep improving. There will also be AI used to control the drones instead of humans. AI is much faster in descisionmaking and reaction time compared to humans, which will result in quick and top quality filming and performance. It will be able to keep track of all players in a playing field. Also the amount of pictures taken to create a film will increase to get smooth video movement.
