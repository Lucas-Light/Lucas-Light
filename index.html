Z Lok

The first reusable zip tie lock. The lightweight and strong Z Lok from Hiplok can easily be carried around. The Z Lok was first created as a lock to secure a bike while sitting nearby. The lock has a stainless steel core that will resist cutting. It can be unlocked with the special key. The Z Lok can also be used to attach accessories securely, such as helmets or other sports equipment.

Diving watch filled with oil

Diving watches are well known for their bulky affairs. It is not just for the looks. The heavy construction is necessary to keep the timepiece from imploding under the pressure in deep waters. This diving watch takes a different approach by taking over the shape inspired by sea turtles. To make the watch more pressure resistant, it is filled with oil. It also makes it more readable underwater when viewed from any angle.

All terrain shoes

These special sneakers are the first all terrain sneakers made. They are supposed to utilize an all terrain vehicle. The 19 nodes on the bototm work like tread tires of an ATV, easily digging through mud, sand and snow to find traction. The nodes in the center are the nodes you run and walk on. The nodes on the side function as training wheels and will keep you from missing your step when climbing rough terrain.
